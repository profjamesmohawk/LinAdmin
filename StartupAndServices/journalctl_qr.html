Quick Reference: journalctl

View all messages 
journalctl
View all messages and follow to see messages as they come in 
journalctl -f
View the last 10 messages 
journalctl -n 10
View messages from the kernel since the last boot 
journalctl -b -k
View messages from the httd.service 
journalctl -u httd.service
